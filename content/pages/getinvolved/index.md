## Get Involved

You can contribute to the standardization efforts in several meaningful ways, including but not limited to:

- Joining the Google Group and sharing your experiences, feedback, and concerns: https://groups.google.com/d/forum/niqc
- Contributing to the various tools listed on the [Tools page](/tools)
- Adding relevant resources on the [Resources page](/resources) by sending a PR or opening an issue on the [niQC GitHub repository](https://github.com/incf/niqc)
- Helping raise awareness about the niQC SIG and promoting its goals within the community.

## Stay Updated

Follow our latest announcements and updates through the [niQC RSS Feed](https://incf.github.io/niQC/rss.xml).
